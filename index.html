About

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur.

Located at De Hemptinneplein 1, 9140 Temse België, right in front of the river, nex to the Giant tree and close to the main buss stop.

Come taste our gourmet hamburgers and our grilled Argentinean and Uruguayan beef. Also find on the menu a wide range of pasta and salads. In the evening, enjoy our cocktails, wines and beers.

Our restaurant can also accommodate large groups of 15 to 100 people for private events (parties, birthdays ...) or business events (seminars, team-buildings, cocktail, reception ...).
